Description:

when you have a family vacation this dish is for you it's not only easy to cook but delicious too.

ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • pork belly
  • water spinach
  • daikon radish
  • sinigang mix
  • garlic
  • onion
  • tomato

Steps:

  1. put garlic and onion
  2. When the garlic turns golden brown, add the tomato
  3. Wait for 20 seconds, then add the pork belly
  4. once the juices begin add 4 cups of water
  5. After adding the water, toss in the daikon radish
  6. Wait until the pork becomes tender, then stir in the sinigang mix
  7. "Once the soup is ready, add the water spinach and cook for another 10 minutes
