Ham and Cheese

HomePage

Description:

The Ham and Cheese Sandwich is a classic, simple recipe that’s great for breakfast, lunch, or a quick snack. With just a few ingredients, you can make a warm and filling sandwich that’s both delicious and easy to prepare. Toasting it makes the cheese melt perfectly for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of Ham
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Spread mayo on the bread (optional)
  2. place hand and cheese between the bread slices
  3. toast on a pan until golden and cheese melts
  4. slice the bread enjoy
