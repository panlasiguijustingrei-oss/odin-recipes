Description

Egg Fried Rice is a quick and easy meal made with simple ingredients you usually have at home. Itâ€™s perfect for using leftover rice and takes only a few minutes to cook. The scrambled eggs, soy sauce, and rice come together to create a tasty and satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (much better leftover)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • salt or magic sarap (to taste)

      • Steps:

      1. Heat oil in a pan
      2. Crack the eggs and scramble them
      3. Add rice and stir well
      4. Pour in soy sauce and mix until rice is evenly coated
      5. season with a little salt or magic sarap and serve hot
